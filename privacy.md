**PlanePlan Privacy Policy**

Last updated: January 2, 2025

**Overview**

PlanePlan is a Chrome extension that filters Google Flights search results by aircraft type. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

**Data Collection**

PlanePlan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension operates entirely within your browser and:
* Does not track your searches or browsing activity
* Does not collect any personal information
* Does not use analytics or tracking tools
* Does not communicate with any external servers

**Data Storage**

PlanePlan stores only your settings preferences (such as whether to show the floating button) locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. This data:
* Never leaves your device
* Is not accessible to PlanePlan or any third party
* Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

**Permissions**

PlanePlan requires the following browser permissions:
* Access to Google Flights pages: To read flight and aircraft information displayed on the page
* Storage: To save your settings preferences locally

**Third-Party Services**

PlanePlan does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

**Changes to This Policy**

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
